About the Wildlife Expedition Tracker

This project was developed as part of the SoftUni Django Basics course. The application serves as a comprehensive tool for managing wildlife data, designed to demonstrate the core functionalities of the Django framework.

Key Features:

  • Animal Database: A centralized registry for different species and their unique characteristics.
  • Expedition Management: Planning and tracking organized field trips to specific locations.
  • Sighting Logs: A detailed record-keeping system for wildlife encounters, including GPS coordinates, timestamps, and photo uploads
